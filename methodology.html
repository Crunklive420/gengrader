How a grade is earned

One rubric, applied the same way to every model. Here is the whole thing — no hidden weighting, no sponsored curves.

The rubric

Five criteria, one overall grade

CriterionWhat we test
Photorealism & render qualityTexture, lighting, anatomy, artifacts at full zoom
Prompt adherenceDoes the output contain what the prompt asked for — subjects, counts, styles, moods
SpeedWall-clock time from prompt to finished image
Text renderingCan it produce readable words inside the image
Cost & accessWhat it actually costs to run, and how easy it is to get access

Each criterion gets a letter grade (A+ through F). The overall grade is our editorial synthesis — speed matters more for a model marketed on speed; text rendering matters more for a model marketed at designers. We say in every review which criteria drove the overall mark.

The evidence standard

Every grade ships with its homework

We generate every sample ourselves, publish the outputs unedited, and host them publicly and permanently on Glipper — so a review can never quietly swap its evidence. What you click is the exact file the model produced. We do not use vendor press images, and we do not retouch outputs.

When we run head-to-head showdowns, every model receives the identical prompt, and we publish the prompt verbatim.

Independence & disclosure

Who pays for this

Nobody. GenGrader is built and funded by the team behind Glipper, an AI media platform. That relationship is disclosed on every page of this site. No AI model vendor pays for placement, grades, or coverage, and being available on Glipper has no effect on a model's grade — the rubric is the rubric.

Where a graded model can be tried for free inside Glipper's Studio, we link to it, because being able to reproduce our results yourself is the strongest check on our honesty.

Corrections

Models change. Grades follow.

AI models are re-trained and re-released constantly. When a model materially changes, we re-run the rubric and re-grade, with the revision date noted on the review. Spot an error? Email glipperapp@gmail.com and we'll investigate.
