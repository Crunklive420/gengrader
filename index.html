Identical prompts, default settings, every generator. No trademarked characters, no real people.

The Rubric

Five weighted marks roll into one letter grade:

Adherence 30%
Quality 25%
Price 20%
Text 15%
Speed 10%

Grades run A+ to F. A means we would hand it to a client. C means usable with rework. F means it failed the assignment.

Our Independence

We buy our own credits and accept no placement fees or free accounts from vendors. Rankings cross-checked against blind-vote arenas (Artificial Analysis, llm-stats) but our grades are our own tests. Every grade carries its test date, and we re-grade within two weeks of a major model release.
