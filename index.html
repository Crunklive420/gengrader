The Rubric

Five weighted marks roll into one score-ring grade:

Adherence 30%
Quality 25%
Price 20%
Text 15%
Speed 10%

A means we would hand it to a client. C means usable with rework. F failed the assignment.

Studies, Not Snapshots

Each study is a dated, frozen evaluation: one suite version, one roster, one evidence set. We open a new study within two weeks of a major model release and quarterly regardless, so no grade ages past ninety days. Superseded studies stay in the archive untouched.

Our Independence

We buy our own credits and accept no placement fees or vendor accounts. Cross-checked against public blind-vote arenas; our grades come from our own suites. All raw evidence is published on Glipper.
